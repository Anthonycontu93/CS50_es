Anthony's journey

I was born in 1993, on November 6th to be exact. In a small town called Castelletto Sopra Ticino. A small country, too small for my dreams. You know, the true beauty of the village and I believe above all of the village in Italy, is life; yes, because life here goes slower, very slowly, people all know each other, one with the other, there are no secrets and no privacy; if someone does something everyone knows, indeed perhaps they know it even before you have done it.

Flying out

My journey in search of me started in 2013, I had never taken a plane, and I did not know a word of English or other languages. I was on an adventure. So, after a roll of the dice, fate chose the day for us to leave; I forgot, there were two of us, my cousin and I, even looking for himself, or looking for whatever we were looking for. Pack your bags, gentlemen.



London, here I am!

Arrived, and like the many stories heard, by others who had already been there, the city welcomed us with a beautiful rain, gray and cloudy sky; the mood was not at its best, but we did not know that from that day on life would change, for the better. The first days all was very complicated, the language, the money, the hostels, the job search, the loneliness, ah yes, my adventure companion left after almost a month, and I stayed there. The obstinacy to find a path of my own, different, out of the predetermined route, it was really a lot, it is so, I didn’t give up.
